About FEAST

Aim

Develop a technology-driven food redistribution system that connects donors, community kitchens, and food seekers via a mobile app and SMS platform.

This system will

  • Identify & distribute surplus food efficiently.
  • Enable real-time coordination between stakeholders.
  • Ensure inclusivity with both online and offline accessibility.
  • Optimize logistics for food pickup and delivery.
  • Promote food safety and responsible donations.

Goal

To reduce food waste, enhance food security, and foster a sustainable, community-driven meal-sharing network.

Community Needs

Access to Nutritious Food

Ensure consistent access to healthy meals for those facing food insecurity.

Food Waste Reduction

Minimize food waste by redistributing surplus food from restaurants, stores, and households.

Real-Time Coordination

Enable seamless communication between donors, kitchens, food seekers, and volunteers.

Scalable & Inclusive

Ensure accessibility in both urban and rural areas, including an SMS-based option.

Efficient Logistics

Optimize food pickup and delivery to ensure quick, safe distribution.

Food Safety & Quality

Maintain food safety standards and address legal concerns for donors.

Community Engagement

Encourage active participation through incentives for donors and volunteers.
